Humble Beginnings

Haley Duran

With the height of the pandemic starting to subside and shops began to open I was dreading the thought of returning to the same limiting life and schedule. With unwavering determination and a burning passion for the entreprenurual journey I meticulously set out to make my dream a reality. After four years of experience under my belt and a solid support system I was well on my way to opening my very first business. We came across some challenges, some financial some structural, but that didn't stop us from perservering. The journey of transforming the building from a boutique to a thriving nail salon was an arduous one. Through tireless work and a commitment to excellence, I watched as all the hard work became an established business in the city I grew up in.
